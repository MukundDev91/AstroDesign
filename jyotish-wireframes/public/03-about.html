Our Mission
हमारा उद्देश्य

Vedic astrology is one of humanity's oldest and most profound wisdom systems. For millennia, it guided kings, shaped empires, and helped ordinary people understand their karma and dharma.

We believe this wisdom should be accessible to everyone — not just those who can afford expensive pandits or have deep Sanskrit knowledge. JyotishAI democratizes authentic Vedic astrology through the power of AI.

ज्योतिष
"Jyotish" means "Science of Light" in Sanskrit. It is the eye of the Vedas — Vedanga Jyotisha — one of the six auxiliary disciplines that illuminate the path of dharma.
